How it works

This tool allows you to upload Calculation Bases for multiple countries at once.

Required CSV Columns:
  • country_code (e.g., AR, GB, ZA)
  • fiscal_year (e.g., 2025)
  • element_code (Must exist in the country)
Logic:

The system will look up the specific Regulation for each row based on the country_code and fiscal_year provided.
